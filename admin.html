Frequently Asked Questions

What career opportunities are available after completing the courses?

+

Our graduates pursue various career paths including:

  • Fashion Designer: Create original clothing designs
  • Boutique Owner: Start your own fashion business
  • Aari Work Specialist: Specialize in traditional embroidery
  • Fashion Entrepreneur: Launch your own fashion brand

What are the prerequisites for enrolling in the courses?

+

No prior experience required. Basic education (10th pass) is preferred. Passion for fashion and creativity is most important.

Do you provide certificates upon course completion?

+

Yes, we provide completion certificates for all our courses. Our diploma course certificate is recognized in the fashion industry.

What is the class size and learning environment?

+

We maintain small batch sizes (8-12 students) to ensure personalized attention and hands-on learning experience.

Do you provide business setup guidance after course completion?

+

We provide guidance for setting up your own business and connect you with our network of alumni and industry contacts.

Can I take multiple courses together?

+

Yes, you can combine courses. We offer flexible scheduling and discounts for multiple course enrollments.
